Mamies World Famous Mac n' Cheese

What you'll need:

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 / 4 cup butter
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 / 2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 / 2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika


Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cook macaroni according to package directions. Drain.
  3. Melt butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Gradually add flour, whisking until well combined. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly until smooth. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick. Put macaroni in large casserole dish, and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.
  4. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add bread crumbs and brown. Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle paprika over top.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Serve.
